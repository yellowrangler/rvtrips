I don't understand the weekly Power rankings and the Team Offense/Defense stats?

This Information displays the Rankings for the team selected, for each week.

The Power Rankings are determined by ESPN's Power Panel (group of 80+ Writers, Editors & TV Personalities) prior to the week’s games.

Offensive Rankings are based on the highest to lowest Average of Yards Gained by each Team.

Defensive Rankings are based on the lowest to highest Average of Yards Gained by the Team’s Opponents.

The Offensive & Defensive Statistical Data is provided by SportsDirect Inc. via Team Rankings, LLC . They are for the previous week’s game.

The charts use reverse power and team rankings. What is that and why?

We feel it is easier to interpret a Line or Bar chart as the higher the line or bar the better the score. This is especially true when comparing bar charts.

There are 32 teams. Normal team and power ranking is from 1 to 32; with 1 being best and 32 being worst. In order to get the graphs to reflect highest line or bar as best; we reverse the rankings.

We subtract each score from 33 thereby flipping the scores and giving us the charting relationships we are looking for! Example: The Patriots are rank 1 in offensive passing. 33 - 1 = 32. That will result in the Patriots at the top of the chart!
